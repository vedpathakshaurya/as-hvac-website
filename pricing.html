Average HVAC Costs

HVAC repair and installation costs vary depending on system type, age, and complexity. Below are typical price ranges for common services:
  • AC Repair: $100 – $450
  • Heating Repair: $120 – $500
  • Furnace Installation: $2,000 – $6,000
  • AC Installation: $3,000 – $8,000
  • Air Quality Systems: $80 – $600

Repair vs Replace

Not sure whether to repair or replace your HVAC system? Here’s a simple guide:
  • Repair: If the system is under 10 years old and the repair cost is under 30% of replacement.
  • Replace: If the system is over 12 years old or requires frequent repairs.
  • Upgrade: If you want better efficiency, lower bills, or improved comfort.
